Some of Nikola Teslas contributions include alternatin curret, the tesla coil, neon lamp, and radio controlled devices. He also created the remote control, laser beams, x-rays, wireless transmissions, etc. All of these still very important in todays society.

Some challenges Nikola faced was his manhattan labratory burning down in a fire which in turn he lost his notes and his prototypes. He also was face with criticism when he revealed the first remote controlled boat where he faced backlash with people calling it fake. He also faced many financial difficulties, fights with investors etc.
